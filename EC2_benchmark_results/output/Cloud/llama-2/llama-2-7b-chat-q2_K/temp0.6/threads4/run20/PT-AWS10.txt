(A) Security Perspective
(B) Governance Perspective
(C) Operations Perspective
(D) Platform Perspective

Answer: 
(A) Security Perspective.

Explanation: The security perspective is crucial when migrating on-premises infrastructure to AWS. This perspective focuses on identifying and addressing potential security risks associated with the migration, such as data breaches or unauthorized access to sensitive information. It also involves implementing security measures during the migration process to ensure that data remains secure in the cloud.
Other perspectives, such as governance, operations, and platform, are important as well. However, security is a critical aspect of the migration process, and addressing security risks proactively can help avoid costly mistakes down the line. By focusing on security early and often during the migration process, an organization can reduce risk and ensure a successful transition to AWS.
