Answer:

Explanation:
The security perspective is crucial for ensuring a secure and efficient migration to AWS. It involves evaluating the company's current security posture, identifying potential threats, and implementing appropriate security controls in AWS. This includes assessing data privacy and integrity, securing network traffic, and implementing identity and access management (IAM) solutions. By focusing on security early in the migration process, the company can avoid potential security issues downstream and ensure a secure cloud infrastructure.
