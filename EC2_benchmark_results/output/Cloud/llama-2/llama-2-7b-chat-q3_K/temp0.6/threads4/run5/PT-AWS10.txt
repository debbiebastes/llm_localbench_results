(A) Security Perspective
(B) Governance Perspective
(C) Operations Perspective
(D) Platform Perspective

Answer: 
(A) Security Perspective.

Explanation: The e-commerce company wants to migrate its on-premise infrastructure to AWS, which involves a significant amount of data transfer and storage. To ensure the migration is secure, the security perspective would be essential in identifying potential vulnerabilities and implementing appropriate security measures to protect the data during the migration process.
The other perspectives (Governance, Operations, and Platform) are important as well, but they primarily focus on ensuring the migration is governed, operated efficiently, and supports the company's business goals, respectively. Security is a critical aspect of the migration that requires careful planning and execution to avoid security breaches or data loss during the transition.
