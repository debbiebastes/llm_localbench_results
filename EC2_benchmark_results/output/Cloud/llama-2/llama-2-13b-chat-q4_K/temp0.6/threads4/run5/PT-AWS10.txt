Answer:

Explanation:

The AWS Cloud Adoption Framework (CAF) provides guidelines for migrating to the cloud, and each perspective focuses on a different aspect of the migration process. The Platform Perspective is concerned with the underlying infrastructure and services that support the workloads being migrated. This includes selecting the appropriate AWS services, designing the architecture, and configuring the environment.

Given that the company wants to migrate its on-premise infrastructure to AWS, the Platform Perspective would be the most essential perspective for their migration. By focusing on the platform perspective, the company can ensure that their workloads are running securely and efficiently in the cloud.
