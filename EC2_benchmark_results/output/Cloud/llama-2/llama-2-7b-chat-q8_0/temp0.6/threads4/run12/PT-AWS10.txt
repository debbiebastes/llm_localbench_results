The Security Perspective (A) would be important for ensuring the security of the data during the migration process, but it may not be as directly relevant to the overall efficiency of the migration. The Platform Perspective (D) focuses on selecting the appropriate AWS services for the migration, but it may not be as relevant to the overall efficiency of the migration process.
