An e-commerce company wants to migrate its on-premise infrastructure to Amazon Web Services. However, they are worried about ensuring their migration is secure and efficient.

Which of the following AWS Cloud Adoption Framework perspective would be the most essential for the company’s migration?

(A) Security Perspective
(B) Governance Perspective
(C) Operations Perspective
(D) Platform Perspective

Answer: 

The correct answer is (D) Platform Perspective. The AWS Cloud Adoption Framework provides a structured approach to migrating applications to the cloud, and the platform perspective focuses on the technical aspects of the migration, such as selecting the appropriate AWS services and tools for the company’s workloads, designing and deploying the infrastructure, and optimizing performance and cost.

The security perspective (A) would be important for ensuring that the company’s data and applications are secure in the cloud, while the governance perspective (B) would help ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and internal policies. The operations perspective (C) would focus on the day-to-day management of the cloud infrastructure and applications.
