Which AWS service enables you to build event-driven applications and decouple the components of your application architecture?

(A) Amazon SQS
(B) Amazon EventBridge (Amazon CloudWatch Events)
(C) Amazon SNS
(D) Amazon Kinesis

Answer: 
(B) Amazon EventBridge (Amazon CloudWatch Events)

Explanation:
EventBridge is a fully managed event bus service that enables you to build event-driven applications and decouple the components of your application architecture. It provides a scalable, reliable, and secure way to integrate with other AWS services and your own custom applications.
