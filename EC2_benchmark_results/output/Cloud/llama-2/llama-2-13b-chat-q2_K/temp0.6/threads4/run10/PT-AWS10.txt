(A) Security Perspective
(B) Governance Perspective
(C) Operations Perspective
(D) Platform Perspective

Answer: 

(A) Security Perspective

Explanation: When migrating to AWS, security is a top priority. The Security Perspective of the AWS Cloud Adoption Framework focuses on protecting sensitive data and assets, complying with regulations, and maintaining privacy. By focusing on security, the company can ensure that its migration is secure and efficient.

The Governance Perspective, Operations Perspective, and Platform Perspective are also important; however, they are less critical during the initial stages of migration.
