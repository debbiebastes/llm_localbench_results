An e-commerce company wants to migrate its on-premise infrastructure to Amazon Web Services. However, they are worried about ensuring their migration is secure and efficient.

Which of the following AWS Cloud Adoption Framework perspective would be the most essential for the company’s migration?

(A) Security Perspective
(B) Governance Perspective
(C) Operations Perspective
(D) Platform Perspective

Answer: 
The (A) Security Perspective would be the most essential for the company’s migration. Ensuring that the data and infrastructure are secure during migration is critical to maintaining the trust and confidence of customers and stakeholders. The security perspective includes measures such as identity and access management, encryption, network security, and compliance with regulatory requirements.
