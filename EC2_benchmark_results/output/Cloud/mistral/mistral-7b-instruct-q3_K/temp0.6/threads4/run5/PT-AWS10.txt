An e-commerce company wants to migrate its on-premise infrastructure to Amazon Web Services. However, they are worried about ensuring their migration is secure and efficient.

Which of the following AWS Cloud Adoption Framework perspective would be the most essential for the company’s migration?

(A) Security Perspective
(B) Governance Perspective
(C) Operations Perspective
(D) Platform Perspective

Answer: 

The most essential perspective for the e-commerce company’s migration to AWS would be the Operations Perspective. The Operations Perspective focuses on ensuring that the infrastructure is running smoothly and efficiently, which is critical when migrating to a new cloud platform. This perspective includes managing applications, services, and data in the cloud, as well as automating processes and monitoring performance. By taking this approach, the e-commerce company can ensure a successful migration while minimizing disruptions to its operations.
