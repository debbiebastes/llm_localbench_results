An e-commerce company wants to migrate its on-premise infrastructure to Amazon Web Services. However, they are worried about ensuring their migration is secure and efficient.

Which of the following AWS Cloud Adoption Framework perspective would be the most essential for the company’s migration?

(A) Security Perspective
(B) Governance Perspective
(C) Operations Perspective
(D) Platform Perspective

Answer: 

The (A) Security Perspective would be the most essential for the company’s migration. As they are concerned about ensuring their migration is secure, this perspective would provide guidance on how to protect their data and applications during and after the migration process. It includes best practices for securing data in transit and at rest, identifying and mitigating risks, implementing security controls, and monitoring and logging.
