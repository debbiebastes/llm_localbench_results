An e-commerce company wants to migrate its on-premise infrastructure to Amazon Web Services. However, they are worried about ensuring their migration is secure and efficient.

Which of the following AWS Cloud Adoption Framework perspective would be the most essential for the company’s migration?

(A) Security Perspective
(B) Governance Perspective
(C) Operations Perspective
(D) Platform Perspective

Answer: 
(C) Operations Perspective.

Rationale: The company is concerned about ensuring their migration is secure and efficient, so the Operations Perspective would be the most essential. This perspective focuses on managing and optimizing the company’s workloads in AWS, including security, networking, and monitoring. It would help the company ensure a smooth and secure migration to AWS while also optimizing its operations in the cloud.
